About Me

Born in Canada, I spent my childhood in England and my teenage years in Florida. I then spent two years in Pennsylvania attending Bryn Athyn College and two more in Illinois attending Northwestern University, from which I graduated with a degree in Economics. For another two years I stayed in the area, working in retail management before relocating to North Carolina to pursue a career that put to use both my creative and problem-solving skills. That desire led me to enroll in the UNC Charlotte Coding Bootcamp.

In my free time I love drawing, writing, and playing sports, including ping pong, volleyball, soccer, and ultimate Frisbee. I also love to travel and can't wait to plan my next trip.
